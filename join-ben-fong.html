Ben is a daily AC Transit rider with a deep connection to the East Bay. Ben’s great-grandparents moved to Oakland in the early 1900’s and his grandmother attended Oakland High School.

Ben has built communities as a former American diplomat, Berkeley Planning Commissioner and transportation expert. Ben will bring his experience combating the 2014 Ebola Outbreak to making AC Transit buses safe.

Endorsements include: Berkeley Democratic Club, Contra Costa Young Dems, East Bay Stonewall Democratic Club, BART Director Rebecca Saltzman, Alameda County Assessor Phong La, Senator Scott Wiener, and over a dozen city councilmembers and officials in Contra Costa County and Alameda County.
